        <h1 class="page-title">The Journey</h1>
        <p>
            Undergoing Rhinoplasty can be a challenging decision. My role as the surgeon is to understand, inform and support you through this process. Your journey can be broken down into three stages- Consultation, Surgery and Aftercare.  
            <br>
            <br>
            At all stages you will be seen by Prof Sama. 
            <br>
            <br>
            <b>Consultation:</b> (Charge Â£250/-) 
            <br>
            <br>
            Undergoing Rhinoplasty procedure can feel like a BIG step. You may have fears and doubts of the possible outcome. The consultation is the most important part of this journey and structured in the following phases.  
            <br>
            <br>
            Phase 1: <b>A face to face consultation.</b>
            <ul>
                <li>Understand your main concerns</li>
                <li>Clinical examination of nose and face</li>
                <li>Preliminary discussion of possible changes that can be made</li>
                <li>Information provided on Rhinoplasty surgery, risks and complication</li>
                <li>Photographs taken</li>
            </ul>
            Phase 2: <b>Review and Morphed images.</b>
            <ul>
                <li>Image morphing technology to communicate realistic changes</li>
                <li>Active involvement in the decision-making process</li>
                <li>Review of previous surgical outcomes</li>
                <li>Morphed images shared electronically</li>
                <li>Quotation for the procedure</li>
            </ul>
            Phase 3: <b>Decision.</b>
            <ul>
                <li>Review and mutual agreement on planned changes</li>
                <li>Specific risks and complications discussed</li>
                <li>Postoperative plan and care discussed</li>
                <li>Potential dates for surgery</li>
            </ul>
            <br>
            <br>
            <b>Surgery:</b> (Cost as quoted at 2nd Consultation- Includes all post operative care for 1 year) 
            <ul>
                <li>Preassessment by hospital team approximately a week prior to surgery (can be face to face or remote)</li>
                <li>Specific instructions regarding the surgery shared by Prof Sama's team (do's and don'ts)</li>
                <li>Seen by Prof Sama and Anaesthetist before and after your surgery</li>
                <li>All postoperative instructions and follow up appointments confirmed</li>
            </ul>
            <br>
            <br>
            <b>Aftercare:</b> (No additional charges related to this)  
            <ul>
                <li>Reviewed at 2 weeks, 2 months, 6 months and 12 months after surgery</li>
                <li>Progress monitored with photographs</li>
                <li>Discharged at 12 months when you are satisfied with the outcome</li>
            </ul>
        </p>
